ABOUT US
      Welcome to our digital herbarium web application! We are dedicated to providing a comprehensive platform for organizing collections of pressed plant specimens and their associated attributes. Our goal is to create a user-friendly experience that allows users to explore, search, and share thematic albums based on multi-criteria searches, such as medicinal plants, mountain flowers, and more.

      With the help of powerful search capabilities, our application enables you to generate personalized albums and recommendations tailored to your specific interests. Whether you're a botany enthusiast, a student, or a nature lover, our platform offers a vast array of botanical representations to satisfy your curiosity.

      To enhance the visual experience, we leverage the public APIs of popular platforms like Unsplash to provide captivating photographs that accompany the plant specimens. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature as you navigate through the rich visual content available.
